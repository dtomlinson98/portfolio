Moderator Script

Welcoming the Participant

Thank you for taking the time to be a part of our usability test for the Snap-on Tool website. We value your expertise and insights as we explore the website's user experience. Please make yourself comfortable, and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions.

Purpose of the Study

Today, we aim to uncover pain-points and gather situational feedback while you interact with the Snap-on Tool website. Your valuable input will help us identify areas for improvement and enhance user satisfaction. We truly appreciate your willingness to participate in this endeavor.

Thinking Out Loud

As you participate, we encourage you to think out loud. Giving audible reactions to your frustrations and triumphs may provide valuable information to our researchers. Capturing your experience in the most raw form is our main goal here. So don't be afradid to voice any thoughts you are having as we conduct this test.

Provide Forms

Before we begin, we kindly request you to sign the consent form, which permits us to record this session for research purposes. Additionally, we have a brief questionnaire to gather situational information that will enrich our understanding of your user experience.

Describe Room

A quick word on the room we will be testing in. This room has been designed to limit distractions and keep your focus on the tasks at hand. Please feel free to get comforable and take breaks as needed.

Testing Process

Throughout the test, we'll present you with a series of tasks to complete on the Snap-on Tool website. Your objective is to explore the website organically, as you would in real-life situations. We're interested in uncovering any pain-points or areas where the website excels. If you encounter any challenges, don't hesitate to shareâ€”it helps us address user needs effectively.

Questions

Before we dive in, do you have any questions or concerns regarding the test's process or any specific aspect of our study? Your comfort and clarity are our top priorities.

Start Study

OK! With all that being said, lets get started. Remember, we're here to understand your situational experience with the Snap-on Tool website. With each task, feel free to share your thoughts and emotions. I'm here to guide and support you throughout the process. Now, let's begin with Task 1.
