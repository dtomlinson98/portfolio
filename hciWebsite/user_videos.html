User Videos

User 1

Pretest Questionnaire

In the pretest questionnaire, user 1 provided insightful feedback about their online tool purchasing habits and preferences. They indicated that they purchase tools online occasionally and are particularly interested in power tools and DIY projects. On a scale of 1 to 5, they felt comfortable using online shopping websites, with a rating of 4. Factors influencing their purchasing decisions included pricing, reviews, and website usability. These responses offer valuable insights into the users' behaviors and expectations.

User 2

Pretest Questionnaire

User 2 expressed that they don't typically purchase tools online. On the scale of 1 to 5, they rated their comfort with online shopping websites as 3, indicating a moderate level of comfort. They have used the tool store website a few times in the past but have not been a frequent visitor. User 2 emphasized the importance of ease of use, pricing, and avaliability when making tool purchases online. Their valuable input provides specific insights into the preferences and experiences of this particular user, which will aid in assessing the website's usability.
