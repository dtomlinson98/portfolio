Two Personas

Randy Highdecker

Demographic:

Randy is a handyman who enjoys working with his hands. He is age 45 and is a homeowner. He values quality, versatility, and function above all else.

Job Title:

Construction Laborer

Goals:

Randy aims to provide top-notch craftsmanship and ensure that his projects meet or exceed his clients' expectations. He strives to complete construction tasks promptly and efficiently, meeting deadlines without compromising on the quality of the work. As a handyman, he seeks opportunities to learn new techniques and stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in the construction industry.

Environment:

Randy frequently works in residential properties, where he tackles a range of tasks, from minor repairs and maintenance to larger remodeling projects. These could include single-family homes, townhouses, apartments, and other types of residential buildings.

Technical Expertise:

Randy is a jack of all trades specializing in tasks related to carpentry, plumbing, electrical, building code, and more.

A word from Randy:

"Through dedication and attention to detail, I bring my best to every project, ensuring my clients are happy with the results." -Randy Highdecker

Kyle Harrison

Demographic:

Kyle is age 38. He is a business owner and places high value on customer service. He is mechanically inclined and is well versed in the products he sells.

Job Title:

Snap On Franchisee

Goals:

As a Snap-on franchisee owner, Kyle's primary goal is to provide exceptional customer service and build long-term relationships with his clients. He aims to meet the diverse tool and equipment needs of automotive professionals and enthusiasts in his region, ensuring they have access to high-quality products.

Environment:

Kyle's environment as a Snap-on franchisee owner who sells tools out of his Snap-on truck is unique and mobile. He operates within a mobile retail setting, allowing him to reach customers directly at their locations, such as automotive repair shops or job sites. This dynamic environment requires him to travel extensively, catering to the tool needs of his customers wherever they are.

Technical Expertise:

Product knowledge, tool application, tool maintenance, and customer assistance.

A word from Kyle:

"As a dedicated Snap-on franchisee, my passion lies in connecting people with the highest quality tools and equipment that elevate their performance and productivity. My goal is to build genuine relationships, supporting professionals and enthusiasts alike in their endeavors, no matter the task they undertake. Together, we strive for excellence, one tool at a time."
